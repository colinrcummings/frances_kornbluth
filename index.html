Artist

Frances Kornbluth (1920 - 2014) was an Abstract Expressionist painter who worked in New York City, Northeastern Connecticut and Monhegan Island, Maine. Her teachers and mentors included Reuben Tam, William Kienbusch and Robert Richenburg.

Artwork

Frances Kornbluth's body of work spans seven decades and can be found in the collecitons of the Chrysler Museum of Art, the Colby College Museum of Art, the Hudson River Museum and the Monhegan Museum.
